Help Center
How do I stake XPL?v
Click Start Staking, enter your amount, connect wallet or send manually, verify, then begin staking.
What is the current APR?v
Current staking APR is 46.09% annually. Rewards accrue every block.
What is the unbonding period?v
When you unstake, there is a 30-day unbonding period. No rewards accrue during this time.
Will my data save if I close the browser?v
Yes — your staked amount and start time are saved locally and restore automatically.
